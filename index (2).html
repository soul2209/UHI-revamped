Live calculation breakdown
Shows exactly how the UHI formula computes temperatures as you move the sliders.
STEP 1 — AMBIENT FLOOR
T_ambient = max(T_max × 0.72, 22)
--
--°C
Minimum temp any zone can reach
STEP 2 — MAX HEAT EXCESS
maxHeat = T_max − T_ambient
--
--°C
Max extra heat urbanization can add
STEP 3 — COOLING RANGE
coolingRange = maxHeat × 0.60
--
--°C
Max cooling green cover can apply
FINAL TEMPERATURE FORMULA (applied per zone)
T_final = max( T_ambient + (maxHeat × U × (1 − G×0.7)) − (model(G) × coolingRange), T_ambient )
